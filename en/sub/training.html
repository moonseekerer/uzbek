IT Education & Instructor Training

Improving Education Quality through Specialized SW Curriculums and Instructor Training (ToT)

To secure educational self-reliance and sustainability in Kashkadarya and Karakalpakstan, we select local teachers, run training of trainers (ToT) programs, and distribute standard curriculums.

Instructor Training (ToT)

We select and train **103 local mentors and instructors** who will teach at the 16 IT education centers. This includes **26 master mentors trained in Korea** through advanced invitation programs. The project supports their local salaries and employment stability to secure permanent, professional education.

Standard SW Curriculums & Textbook Development

We develop custom textbooks and curriculums in Korean, English, and Uzbek, tailored to local needs. We target training **150 pilot students in 2028**, and scaling up to **1,200 students per year from 2029 to 2032**.

💻 Basic SW Course

  • • Block Coding: Scratch & Entry (Logic building)
  • • Web Development: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript basics
  • • IT Literacy: Basic computer literacy and OS management

🚀 Advanced SW Course

  • • Python Programming: Data structures & basic algorithms
  • • Mobile App Development: App building tools and UI design
  • • 3D Maker Education: 3D printing and modeling basics
