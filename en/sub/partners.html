Partnership

Tight Mutual Collaboration Model among Leading Professional Institutions

This project is executed efficiently and transparently under the financial support of KOICA (Korea International Cooperation Agency), and is jointly driven by the PMC consortium and the Ministry of Digital Technologies of Uzbekistan.

KOICA

Supporting & Supervising Agency

- Provides grant ODA funding for the implementation of the project.
- Coordinates standard inter-governmental agreements (MOU/RD) and oversees overall performance.
- Approves main budget executions and supplies core ODA policy guidelines.

PMC Consortium

PMC Consortium (Gyeongbuk Technopark Consortium)

Gyeongbuk Technopark (Lead Partner)

Manages overall project execution (PMC), supervises center remodeling construction, coordinates inter-governmental agendas, and conducts policy consultations.

Korea Productivity Center (Co-Partner)

Develops IT curricula and textbooks, establishes the Instructor training model (ToT), operates invitation training in Korea, and runs satisfaction assessments.

Global Telecom (Co-Partner)

Designs network systems for centers, procures high-spec hardware (PCs, laptops, smartboards), and supervises local installation.

위밸류에이트 (Co-Partner)

Manages project logical frame (PDM) indicators, performs baseline/midterm/endline evaluations, and checks the socio-economic sustainability impact.

Ministry of Digital Tech

Recipient Government Partner (Ministry of Digital Technologies)

- Provides land/facilities in the 16 districts for the education centers, securing administrative permits.
- Promotes class enrollment, recruits local instructors, and coordinates local government actions.
- Manages independent operations post-project, providing center salaries and utility costs for long-term sustainability.

UZB YOUTH IT PMC
Promoting Employment and Entrepreneurship Capability through Improving the Youth IT Education Ecosystem in Uzbekistan

This project is funded by KOICA and executed under the supervision of the Ministry of Digital Technologies of Uzbekistan to establish digital education equality and activate the youth career ecosystem.
