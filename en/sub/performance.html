📊 IT Infrastructure & Equipment Supply Roadmap (PDM standard)

Phase-by-phase plans for building remodeling (Activity 1.1) and equipment procurement support (Activity 1.2) specified in the PDM.

Activity 1.1: Digital Education Center Building & Remodeling Phase
Baseline 2025~2026
0 Centers (Preparation)
Property suitability surveys and blueprint design reviews completed for all 16 target centers.
Midline 2027~2028
Remodeling & Construction
2 Hub centers remodeled (Q3 2027) & 14 Local centers built (Q4 2028), preparing for co-management.
Endline 2032
16 Centers Self-Operated
Uzbekistan government budget secured for 16 IT centers to achieve 100% self-reliance.
Activity 1.2: IT Equipment Procurement & Standard Supply Phase
Baseline 2025~2026
Procurement Rate: 0%
Draft specifications prepared for standard IT equipment, supporting PIU procurement review.
Midline 2027~2028
100% Supplied & Installed
Delivery, assembly, and joint inspections completed for all 16 centers (Q4 2028).
Endline 2032
Self-Reliant Cycles
Secured host country operational budgets for spare parts, upgrades, and replacement cycles.
