IT Infrastructure

Establishing Standard Digital Infrastructure for Youth in Kashkadarya and Karakalpakstan

This project designs and constructs KOICA Youth Digital Technology Education Centers in 16 target districts across Kashkadarya Region and the Republic of Karakalpakstan. We remodel existing facilities, build new schools, and procure high-quality multimedia equipment packages.

Education Center Construction (2 Hubs, 14 Locals)

With a budget of 5,934.0 million KRW ($4.3 million), we perform **eco-friendly remodeling of 2 Hub-type centers** and coordinate the **new construction of 14 Local-type centers**. The facilities feature environment-friendly thermal insulation, high-efficiency HVAC heating/cooling, open collaboration spaces, and high-speed internet.

Standard IT Equipment Packages (H-type 17 items / L-type 8 items)

Using a budget of 3,726.0 million KRW ($2.7 million), we procure custom equipment packages tailored to the center sizes. Going beyond mere PC donations, we supply hardware capable of running advanced programming and data analysis tools, while training local operations teams in maintenance.

🌟 Hub-type Equipment Package (17 items)

  • • High-spec desktops for multimedia design
  • • Instructor/presentation laptops
  • • 75-inch large smart electronic boards
  • • NAS servers & network rack systems
  • • Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) units
  • • 3D printers & SW maker education kits

🌱 Local-type Equipment Package (8 items)

  • • Student laptops for coding & web-building
  • • Lecture projectors & screens
  • • Wireless Wi-Fi Access Points & switches
  • • Multi-function laser printers & consumables
  • • Unstable grid protection power regulators
  • • Entry-level coding kits & programming boards
