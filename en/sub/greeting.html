Greeting

Supporting the endless dreams and challenges of Uzbekistan youth.

Dear visitors,

Uzbekistan is a dynamic country with a rich history and culture, where over 60% of the population consists of youth. Recently, the government of Uzbekistan has been actively promoting national digital transformation policies, focusing all efforts on economic growth and youth job creation.

However, youth in rural and remote regions, excluding the capital Tashkent, still face digital alienation due to poor IT educational infrastructure.

Therefore, with the funding of the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA), our project team is faithfully executing the 'Project for Promoting Employment and Entrepreneurship Capability through Improving the Youth IT Education Ecosystem in Uzbekistan'. We aim to go beyond simply donating hardware to establish a sustainable digital cycle by providing tailored IT training, fostering local instructors, and linking education to actual job creation and startup opportunities.

We ask for your warm interest and support so that the youth of Uzbekistan can fly high toward the global stage with the wings of digital technology.

Kim Yang-jin, Project Manager (PM)
Project for Promoting Employment and Entrepreneurship Capability through Improving the Youth IT Education Ecosystem in Uzbekistan

Core Values

  • Digital Equality: Overcoming geographical and economic limits to provide equal digital opportunities.
  • Local Empowerment: Securing educational sustainability through local instructor training (ToT).
  • Career Connection: Moving beyond basic learning to match students with corporate jobs and technology startups.
UZB YOUTH IT PMC
Promoting Employment and Entrepreneurship Capability through Improving the Youth IT Education Ecosystem in Uzbekistan

This project is funded by KOICA and executed under the supervision of the Ministry of Digital Technologies of Uzbekistan to establish digital education equality and activate the youth career ecosystem.
