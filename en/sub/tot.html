Teacher & Operator Training

Empowering local teachers (ToT), center managers, and policy makers for sustainable IT education

To establish a self-sustaining educational system without relying on short-term dispatches, we run systematic Training of Trainers (ToT) programs including Korea invitation training for 26 master mentors, local training for 416 instructors, 10 policy makers, and 48 center managers.

🇰🇷 Output 3.1.1. Master Mentor Korea Invitation Training (26 experts)

Inviting 26 selected Uzbek instructors to Korea to transfer advanced IT teaching methodologies, curriculum design, and hands-on lab techniques.

  • • Curriculum Mastery: Intensive training based on the 17 developed IT modules
  • • Official Certification: Joint certificates issued by KOICA and MDT
  • • Local Knowledge Transfer: Leading local instructor mentoring upon return to Uzbekistan

🏫 Output 3.1.2 & 3.1.3. Local ToT Training (442 instructors)

• Master Mentor Local Workshops (3.1.2): 2 sessions of local reinforcement for 26 master mentors
• General Instructor Local ToT (3.1.3): 6 sessions training 416 regional instructors to teach at 16 centers

🏛️ Output 3.2. Policy Maker & Center Manager Training (58 leaders)

• Policy Maker Invitation Training (3.2.1): 10 senior MDT officials invited to Korea for youth IT masterplan training
• Center Manager Local Training (3.2.2): 5 sessions for 48 center managers on administration and financial operations
