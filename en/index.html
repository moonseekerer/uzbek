KOICA ODA PROJECT (2026-2032)

Empowering youth in Uzbekistan
with a new digital future

We establish 16 IT education centers in Kashkadarya and the Republic of Karakalpakstan to bridge the digital gap and enhance youth employment and entrepreneurship capabilities.

Project Overview Contact Us

From IT Infrastructure
to Jobs & Startups

We support everything from building 2 Hub and 14 Local centers, training 103 local instructors (ToT), to providing startup mentoring and university entrance consulting.

Key Projects Activity Gallery

Key Project Areas

01

IT Infrastructure

We establish youth digital technology education centers across 16 districts in remote areas of Uzbekistan, supplying state-of-the-art educational hardware.

  • Hub-type center remodeling (2 locations)
  • Local-type center construction (14 locations)
  • Custom equipment supply (17 items for H-type / 8 items for L-type)
02

IT Education & ToT

To ensure educational self-reliance, we select local teachers, train them through our ToT program, and distribute advanced SW curricula.

  • Developing standard IT curricula & textbooks
  • Training 103 local master mentors and teachers (ToT)
  • Invitation training in Korea for master mentors
03

Career & Startup Support

We provide mentoring, hold hackathons, match students with internships, and consult them on entering IT-related university departments.

  • Regular career/startup workshops & consulting
  • Matching students with corporate internships & job fairs
  • University IT department tours & educational guidance

Our Goal & Impact

Realizing digital equality
to bridge the urban-rural gap.

We eliminate the digital divide for youth in Kashkadarya and Karakalpakstan, linking IT education to actual job creation and technological startups for sustainable development.
